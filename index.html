About Me

I’m a UX Designer with a passion for creating meaningful, human-centered, and ethical digital experiences. I’m currently studying Web Design & Development at Utah Valley University, where I’m building a strong foundation in UX strategy, visual design, and front-end development. I enjoy being involved in every stage of the design process, and I especially love collaborating with others to bring thoughtful ideas to life. I dabble in code here and there, which helps me better understand what’s possible and design with implementation in mind.

At the core of my work is a curiosity about people—how they think, what they need, and how design can genuinely improve their everyday experiences. I’m drawn to projects that prioritize clarity, accessibility, and ethical decision-making, and I’d consider myself an enthusiastic life-long learner.

Outside of design, I’m creative in just about everything I do. I’ve been playing clarinet for nearly a decade, and I enjoy attending band class with my brother. I also love drawing and character design, and I write poetry whenever inspiration strikes. When I’m not designing, you’ll probably find me sketching, reading comics, or spending time with friends and family.
